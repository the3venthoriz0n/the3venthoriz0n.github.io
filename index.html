The Band

Mama and the Stops is a soulful band from the acclaimed Conservatory of Music at SUNY Purchase. Their honest expressive sound is comparable to that of Allen Stone, Amy Winehouse and Lake Street Dive. At a Mama and the Stops show you will always find yourself among a dancing crowd. Their rich harmonies, and compelling melodic renditions of crowd favorites and original compositions will make you feel at home even among strangers. They’ve crafted a repertoire that swings between vulnerability and joy, hope and revelry. Each member’s unique musical background makes Mama and the Stops a truly eclectic group. Their roots range from Jazz, and R&B, to SingerSongwriter and Pop. Members have played at venues such as Pianos, Birdland, Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center and at the New York City Couture Fashion Week.

Leah Woods: Lead vocals and ukulele.

Corry Loveday: Lead vocals and melodica.

Jack Redsecker: Keyboard, keytar, and vocals.

Andrew Kaplan: Drums, guitar, and vocals.

The East Coast Tour

In May 2016, Mama and the Stops embarked on a two-week tour down the East coast. Along the way they promoted awareness and collected donations for women’s shelters, homeless shelters, and arts organizations in the communities they visited. They raised over four thousand dollars!
